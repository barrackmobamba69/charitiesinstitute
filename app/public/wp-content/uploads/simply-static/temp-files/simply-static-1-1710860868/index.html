Charities Institute Ireland: Leading the Way for High-Impact Charities

The Charities Institute Ireland (CII) stands as the preeminent professional body for Ireland's most impactful fundraising charities. Representing over 280 members and a substantial share of public funding within the sector, CII fosters a culture of transparency, best practices, and maximizing the transformative power of charities in Irish society.
